A gliding flight has 0 thrust,which has a drag of
\[
D=W\sin\theta_g
\]

When climbing, the drag is
\[
D=-W\sin\theta_c
\]

With thrust, the drag is
\[
D=T - W\sin\theta_c
\]

Solving for T gives us
\[
T=D+W\sin\theta_c
\]
